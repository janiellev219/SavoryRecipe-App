Cheese Fondue


Serves 5, Time 20 minutes

Ingredients 1 cup dry white wine 1/2 pound shredded Swiss cheese 1/2 pound shredded Gruyere cheese 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 1/4 teaspoon salt Â¼ teaspoon nutmeg 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg 1 (1 pound) loaf French bread, cut into 1 inch cubes

Step by Step

  1. Simmer wine in fondue pot. Add Swiss cheese, Gruyere cheese, 1/4 pound at a time. Stir after each addition of cheese until melted. Stir in flour. When all the cheese has melted, stir in salt and nutmeg. Serve with cut-up French bread.
